Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers try to trick you into giving sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, or personal details by pretending to be a trusted entity.

Real Scenario:

Alex received an email pretending to be his bank asking him to verify his account. He almost entered his password before noticing the suspicious URL.

Solution:

  • Check sender carefully
  • Hover over links to verify URL
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Report phishing emails
